EqualMath Privacy Policy

Protecting your privacy is our priority

Last updated

August 20, 2025

Last effective date

August 20, 2025

1. Data collection

EqualMath (version 1.0.1) is a completely local application that does not collect any personally identifiable information. The app does not collect your name, email address, contact information, or device location information. All operations are completed on your device, and no data is transmitted to the server.

2. Data storage

All user-created data, including geometric shapes, calculation processes, and settings, are stored in the local SQLite database of your device. These data are completely retained on your device and will not be uploaded to any cloud service, nor will they be accessed by developers or any third parties. If you uninstall the app, all locally stored data will be permanently deleted.

3. Access to photo albums

The app only needs to access your photo album in the following situations: when you choose to export geometric shapes as images and save them to the photo album, or when you choose to import images from the photo album into the app. The app will not access your photo album in the background, nor will it collect or upload any of your photos. All access to the photo album is only performed with your explicit action. Images uploaded to the app will be stored in the user's local SQLite database, and users can delete them at any time or delete them along with the app upon uninstallation.

4. Data security

We place great importance on your data security: all data is stored encrypted locally on the device, and the app does not contain any advertisements or analytical tracking. No third-party SDKs will access your data. Although the app itself does not transmit data, we recommend that you keep your device operating system updated, use device passwords or biometric protection, and avoid installing apps on untrusted devices.

5. Children's privacy

This app is suitable for users of all ages, but we pay special attention to children's privacy: the app does not collect any personal information from children, has no social functions or user interaction, and all content is educational geometry drawing tools. Children under 13 should use this app under parental guidance.

6. Policy updates

We may occasionally update this Privacy Policy. The updated policy will be prominently displayed within the app, including the new update date. By continuing to use the app, you are accepting the updated policy.
